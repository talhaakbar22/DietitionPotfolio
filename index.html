Services

Nutrition Assessment

Dietitians conduct comprehensive assessments of individuals' dietary habits, lifestyle factors, medical history, and nutritional status to identify areas for improvement and develop tailored nutrition plans.

Nutrition Counseling

Dietitians provide one-on-one counseling sessions to help individuals set realistic nutrition goals, make informed food choices, and adopt healthy eating behaviors.

Fitness Program

Our Fitness Program is tailored to meet the unique needs and preferences of each individual, focusing on improving physical fitness, enhancing overall well-being, and promoting a sustainable, healthy lifestyle.

Medical Nutrition Therapy

Dietitians work with individuals managing chronic diseases or medical conditions such as diabetes, cardiovascular disease, hypertension, gastrointestinal disorders, kidney disease, and food allergies. They develop specialized nutrition plans to help manage symptoms, optimize treatment outcomes, and improve health.

Eating Disorder Therapy

Dietitians collaborate with mental health professionals in the treatment of eating disorders such as anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa, binge eating disorder, and orthorexia. They provide nutrition therapy to support physical and psychological recovery, normalize eating behaviors, and restore nutritional health.

Pediatric Nutrition

Dietitians specialize in pediatric nutrition and offer guidance to parents and caregivers on promoting healthy growth and development in infants, children, and adolescents. They address issues such as breastfeeding, formula feeding, introduction of solids, picky eating, and managing childhood obesity.
